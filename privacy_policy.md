# Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy and only collect what is necessary for the app to work.

## What data we collect
We collect a small amount of information:
- A username chosen by you
- A password (stored only as a secure hash, never in plain text)
- A system-generated user ID
- Confirmation that you agreed to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy

We do not collect real names, email addresses, or any other personal details.

## Why we collect this data
We use this data to:
- Identify you as a user in the system
- Secure your account
- Let you manage your personal book collection

## How your data is stored and protected
All data is stored in memory on the server.
Passwords are hashed before being stored so they cannot be read or recovered.

## Deleting your data
You can delete your account at any time.
When you do:
- Your user account is removed
- Your consent is withdrawn
- All personal data related to your account is deleted

## Your rights
You have the right to:
- Know what data is stored about you
- Delete your account and all associated data

## Consent
By creating an account, you actively agree to this Privacy Policy.
If you do not agree, no account is created.
